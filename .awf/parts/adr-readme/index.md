## INDEX.md

`INDEX.md` is a generated index: **do not edit it by hand**. It is regenerated by `awf sync`
(here, `./x sync`) from the ADRs, and `awf check` (`./x check`, run by the pre-commit hook)
fails if it is stale. It lists Proposed, Accepted, and Implementing ADRs under *In flight* and Implemented and
Abandoned ADRs under a compact *History* by number, title, and status. After adding an ADR or
changing a `status:` field, run `./x sync` and stage the regenerated `INDEX.md` alongside your
change.
